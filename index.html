Hey, I’m Joe!

I’m a recent graduate from Michigan State University with a Bachelor of Arts degree. At MSU, I majored in Media and Information with a focus on web design and development and minored in computer science. I began college in engineering, but quickly developed a passion for designing and building websites and web applications.

Throughout my time at MSU, I took all the available web design and development courses, plenty of computer science courses, as well as courses on digital business, user experience and data visualization (Click to View). Beyond my learning at MSU, I've taught myself a great deal and taken two full-stack web development courses on Udemy.

I gained a lot of experience from my time at MSU working in and leading teams to build applications using Scrum as a development cycle framework. This improved my organization, communication, and code readability skills. These abilities have lent themselves to my solo projects as well, improving my overall efficiency and workflow.

Since my graduation in May, I've been continuing to learn by teaching myself new skills and improving my existing ones. At the same time, I've been designing and building my portfolio/personal website and working with the EMU Science department in planning and designing a website for its students to use as a place for shared learning and discussion.

Web Design and Development (HTML, CSS, Git), Advanced Web Development and Databases (HTML, CSS, SCSS, JS), Web Application Architecture & Development (HTML, CSS, SCSS, PHP, SQL, AJAX, JS), Introduction to Programming I (Python), Introduction to Programming II (C++), Discrete Structures Comp Sci (Discrete Mathematics), Software Design (OOP centered design in C++), Digital Business, Innovative Interfaces, Media Sketching & Graphics, Design and Layout, Evaluating HCT (Emphasis on usability) and Reasoning with Data (RStudio data visualization).
